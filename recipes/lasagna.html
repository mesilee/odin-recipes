Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is rich, cheesy, and delicious. A comfort food classic!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Cook beef with sauce
  3. Layer noodles, meat, and cheese
  4. Bake for 45 minutes
